Quiz Maker FAQs

What file types does Quiz Maker support?
Quiz Maker supports PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, and RTF files up to 100MB.
How many questions can I generate?
You can generate up to 50 questions per quiz. Customize the number and types of questions.
Is Quiz Maker free to use?
Yes! Quiz Maker is free to use with no download required. Premium features available.
Can I share quizzes with others?
Absolutely! Share quizzes via link or invite classmates directly to your study space.
How is my data handled?
Your files are securely processed by Adobe servers and deleted unless you sign in.
